## 📋 我们的承诺 Our Pledge

作为贡献者和维护者，我们承诺为所有人提供一个欢迎和友好的体验，无论年龄、体型、残疾、民族、性别认同和表达、经验水平、教育程度、社会经济地位、国籍、个人外貌、种族、宗教或性取向。

As contributors and maintainers, we pledge to make participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## 📝 我们的标准 Our Standards

有助于创造积极环境的行为包括：

Behavior that contributes to a positive environment includes:

- ✅ **使用欢迎和包容的语言 Using welcoming and inclusive language**
- ✅ **尊重不同的观点和经历 Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences**
- ✅ **优雅地接受建设性批评 Gracefully accepting constructive criticism**
- ✅ **关注对社区最有利的事情 Focusing on what is best for the community**
- ✅ **对其他社区成员表示同理心 Showing empathy towards other community members**

不可接受的行为包括：

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- ❌ **使用性化语言或图像以及性骚扰 Using sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances**
- ❌ **发布侮辱性评论 Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks**
- ❌ **公开或私下骚扰 Public or private harassment**
- ❌ **未经明确许可发布他人的私人信息 Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or electronic address**
- ❌ **在专业场合被合理认为不适当的其他行为 Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting**

## 🔍 执行范围 Scope

本行为准则适用于所有项目空间，并且当个人代表项目或其社区时也适用于其他地方。代表项目或社区的示例包括使用官方电子邮件地址、通过官方社交媒体帐户发帖或在在线或 offline 活动中担任指定代表。维护者可以定义和澄清项目代表范围。

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, and it also applies when an individual represents the project or its community in public spaces. Examples of representing a project or community include using an official e-mail address, posting via an official social media account, or acting as an appointed representative at an online or offline event. Representation of a project may be further defined and clarified by project maintainers.

## 🚨 执行 Enforcement Enforcement

 instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project team at admin@0379.email. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

All community leaders are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.

### **执行指南 Enforcement Guidelines**

社区负责人将遵循这些社区影响准则来确定他们认为违反本行为准则的任何行为的后果：

Community Leaders will follow these Community Impact Guidelines in determining the consequences for any action they deem in violation of this Code of Conduct:

#### **1. 修正性回应 Corrective Action**

**社区影响 Community Impact**: 使用不当语言或其他被认为不专业或不适合其所在社区的行为

**后果 Consequence**: 对违规者发出书面警告，并说明违反了什么行为。要求公开道歉。

#### **2. 警告 Warning**

**社区影响 Community Impact**: 通过单一事件或一系列行为违反此行为准则

**后果 Consequence**: 持续时间不超过警告的制裁。不允许与相关人员互动，包括就与社区负责人有关的事项进行非结构化的沟通。这包括在社交媒体渠道上互动。违反这些条款可能导致临时或永久禁令。

#### **3. 临时禁令 Temporary Ban**

**社区影响 Community Impact**: 严重违反社区标准，包括持续的不当行为

**后果 Consequence**: 临时禁止与社区进行任何形式的互动或公开评论，为期不超过一周。在此期间不得与社区人员公开或私下联系。Rationale 包括在公共通知中。

#### **4. 永久禁令 Permanent Ban**

**社区影响 Community Impact**: 违反行为准则的模式，包括持续的不当行为、骚扰个人或攻击或贬低一类人

**后果 Consequence**: 社区的永久禁止任何公开互动。

## 📞 报告 Reporting

如果您遇到或目睹了违反本行为准则的行为，请向项目管理团队报告：

If you encounter or witness a violation of this Code of Conduct, please report it to the project management team:

**联系方式 Contact Information:**
- 📧 **Email**: admin@0379.email
- 📱 **GitHub**: 通过 [Issue](https://github.com/YYC-Cube/YYC3-Financial-Dashboard/issues) 报告（标记为 `security` 或 `conduct`）

**请包含 Please include:**
- 📝 您的联系方式 Your contact information
- 👤 被举报人的账户信息 (if applicable) Account information of the person you're reporting
- 📍 发生地点 Where the incident occurred
- ⏰ 发生时间 When the incident occurred
- 📄 详细描述 Detailed description of the incident
- 📎 证据（截图、链接等） Evidence (screenshots, links, etc.)

我们承诺：

We commit to:
- ⏱️ 在收到报告后 48 小时内确认 Acknowledge receipt within 48 hours
- 🔒 保护您的隐私 Protect your privacy and confidentiality
- 📊 进行彻底调查 Conduct a thorough investigation
- 💬 提供更新和结果 Provide updates on progress and outcome
- 🛡️ 防止报复 Prevent retaliation against reporters

## 🌐 多样性与包容性 Diversity & Inclusion

YYC³ 项目致力于创建一个多元化和包容性的环境：

The YYC³ project is committed to creating a diverse and inclusive environment:

### **我们的价值观 Our Values**

- 🌈 **包容性 Inclusivity** - 欢迎来自不同背景的人
